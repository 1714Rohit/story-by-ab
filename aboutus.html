About Stories by AB

What began as a hobby for Akash Bawaskar, a self-taught photographer with a creative eye, has grown into a vision-driven brand committed to storytelling through photos and films. Akash founded Stories by AB with a simple goal — to deliver experiential visuals that truly resonate with clients. Backed by a team of passionate professionals, we ensure that every photograph and video we deliver doesnt just capture an event, but tells a heartfelt story.
